Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about shopping on MtcSHOPPING

MtcSHOPPING is the leading online marketplace that connects you with local stores and restaurants for same-day delivery. We bring together groceries, food, electronics, fashion, beauty products, and much more from trusted stores and restaurants across the city.

We offer same-day delivery. Most orders from nearby stores or restaurants arrive within 30 minutes to a few hours, depending on your location and the store's proximity.

We accept MTN Mobile Money (MoMo), Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Apple Pay, and cash on delivery.
